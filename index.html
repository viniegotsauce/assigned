About Routelli

Routelli is a renowned chef from the early 70's who discovered various ways of preparing some of the worlds finest dishes from an early age. He opened his first resturant in 1979 where he served a total of four dishes. After his death in 2002, the resturant was taken over by his family and they upgraded the resturant further. A number of dishes were added to the menu.
We welcome you to our Resturant any time, feel at home as we serve you with our tasty dishes.
